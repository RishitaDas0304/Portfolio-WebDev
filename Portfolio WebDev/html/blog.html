A few of my favourite things

Baking

I have been very enthusiastic about baking since a very young age. It has been the influence of my mother, who is a professional baker. I really enjoy whipping up sweet treats for my family as a small gesture, every now and then. Now that my mother and I are like friends, I often like to help her out with the decorations for big cakes!

Music

I hardly know where to start when it comes to this topic. More than a hobby, music is my passion. I have been a classical dancer, learnt how to play the synthesiser and have a history of music in my family. Thus, it is safe to say that I have a massive incline towards music, starting from The Shadows, Queen, Nirvana to One Direction, Arctic Monkeys and Taylor Swift, I know the lyrics like the back of my hand!


Badminton

A hobby I have a love/ hate relationship with. I started learning the game at the young age of 8 and continued ever since. The reason I say I hate the game is because of the 7 am classes I had every Sunday morning! Otherwise, I am fanatic about it. Anytime I need a breather, a break from the hustle and bustle of life, a badminton court is where you will find me!
